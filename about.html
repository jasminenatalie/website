Hello!

I’m Jasmine Natalie but you can call me Jas ;>

I am an art student, fashion designer, and writer.

View My CV


I taught myself from a very young age how to draw stick figures and cartoons not thinking too much about it.
Not long after, little me was introduced to a whole new world of creative opportunities and my first dream of being a graphic designer was born.




I was able to fulfill that hobby by joining organizations such as Bye Bye Plastic Bags as a graphic designer. I became familiar with the creative workflow and am now able to work under pressure in a fast-paced environment without sacrificing the quality of my art.

Portfolio

My heart was set on going to design school and becoming a graphic designer. Then when quarantine hit, I came across Next in Fashion and was instantly hooked by this part of the design world I’ve never considered being in. That moment was when I knew it was what I wanted to do.
